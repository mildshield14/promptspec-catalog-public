## What Is Included

- Pattern definitions derived from published prompt-engineering literature
- Structural metadata (categories, subcategories, detection heuristics)

## What Is NOT Included

- **No raw production prompts** from any corpus or dataset
- **No company names**, client names, or organizational identifiers
- **No workflow names** or proprietary business process identifiers
- **No prompt IDs** from any production system
- **No proprietary business logic** or trade secrets
- **No confidential annotation data** or annotator identifiers
- **No corpus frequency statistics** tied to specific prompts or clients

## Data Provenance

All pattern definitions are derived from published prompt-engineering literature. They were not empirically observed in a prompt corpus, and this repository does not include corpus-derived provenance labels.
